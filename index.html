How to subscribe

Everything you need to do,
in exact order

From zero to your first digest in under 10 minutes. No technical knowledge required.

1

Choose your plan

~1 minute
  • › Click below to go directly to the early access checkout — $1.97/mo, no plan comparison needed
  • › Not sure which to pick? Start with Essential — you can upgrade at any time with no interruption to your digest
  • › Click Get early access to proceed to payment
2

Complete payment via Stripe

~2 minutes · 100% secure
  • › You will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page — the industry standard for online payments
  • › Enter the e-mail address where you want to receive your digests — this will be your account identifier
  • › Enter your card details — Stripe processes everything directly; we never see or store your card information
  • › Your subscription starts immediately at $1.97/mo — cancel anytime from your Stripe dashboard
  • › Stripe sends you a confirmation e-mail with your receipt and subscription details
3

Fill in your subscriber profile

~3 minutes · defines your filter
  • › Open the registration form — use the exact same e-mail you used on the Stripe payment page
  • › Select your domain and field, then add your keywords — we automatically map the most relevant subfields and journals from your inputs
  • › Add your keywords of interest (up to 10 on Essential, up to 20 on Professional) — the more specific, the better. Examples: "CRISPR", "lung adenocarcinoma", "seismic inversion", "transformer architecture"
  • › Optionally add blacklist keywords — any article title containing these words will be excluded from your digest, no matter what
  • › Optionally refine the suggested subfields and journals — pre-selected based on your keywords, you can remove anything that doesn't fit
  • › Submit the form — your profile is saved and your filter is active from the next weekly run
4

Receive your first digest

Every Friday · automatic
  • › Your first digest arrives on the next Friday after your profile is complete — no action needed on your part
  • › Each digest covers articles published in the previous 7 days that matched your field, subfield, and keywords
  • › For each article you will see: title, authors, journal, publisher, publication date, and a direct DOI link to the source
  • › If results feel too broad or too narrow, simply return to the registration form, update your preferences, and resubmit — changes take effect on the very next send

FAQ

Common questions

Research Scout sends digests every Friday evening. Each edition covers articles published during the previous 7 days across indexed scholarly sources — CrossRef and OpenAlex.
If no exact matches are found, Research Scout automatically expands the discovery scope and surfaces related high-relevance publications from adjacent topics — so every digest remains valuable, even in quieter publishing weeks.
We index CrossRef and OpenAlex — two of the largest open scholarly metadata repositories. Together they cover tens of millions of articles across all scientific disciplines, with new publications added continuously.
Our pipeline runs weekly and captures articles published in the 7 days prior to each Friday send. Most articles appear within 24–48 hours of their official publication date.
